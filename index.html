Welcome

The Massachusetts Computer Science Olympiad is a high-school programming competition hosted by students at Brookline High. We create coding questions focusing on clever techniques, and students of all programming backgrounds are welcome to participate!

Meet the Team

We are a group of Brookline High School students passionate about computer science. We wish people could network and have fun through our coding competition. Near Brookline, many excellent universities exist, such as Boston College, Boston University, MIT, and Harvard.
